About us

Art of Siam Reading, a second branch of our popular authentic Thai cuisine first established in 2002. The restaurant is located on the first floor of The Walk shopping arcade, one of the most famous eating out place in Reading situated next to The Oracle, Reading town Centre. The restaurant has a capacity of 65 cozy seating to ensure a worm Thai home style service.
